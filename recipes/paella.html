Paella

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 4 cups of chicken broth
  • 1 cup of saffron threads
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1 cup of peas
  • 1 cup of artichoke hearts, quartered
  • 200g of chicken breast, cubed
  • 200g of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 200g of mussels, cleaned
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan or paella dish over medium heat.
  2. Add the onion and garlic, and cook until softened.
  3. Add the bell peppers and tomato, and cook for another minute.
  4. Add the rice and saffron, and stir to coat the grains.
  5. Add the chicken broth gradually, stirring constantly.
  6. Cover and simmer for about 20 minutes or until the rice is cooked.
  7. In a separate pan, cook the chicken breast until done. Remove and set aside.
  8. In the same pan, cook the shrimp until pink. Remove and set aside.
  9. In the same pan, cook the mussels until they open. Remove and set aside.
  10. Gently fold in the cooked chicken, shrimp, mussels, peas, and artichoke hearts into the paella.
  11. Sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.

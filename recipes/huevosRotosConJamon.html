Huevos Rotos con Jamón

Ingredients:

  • 4 eggs
  • 200g of jamón serrano (Spanish cured ham)
  • 4 medium potatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Instructions:

  1. Peel and cut the potatoes into thin slices.
  2. Heat olive oil in a frying pan and fry the potatoes until golden and crispy. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  3. In the same pan, fry the eggs sunny side up.
  4. Slice the jamón serrano into thin strips.
  5. To serve, place the fried potatoes on a plate, top with the fried eggs, and sprinkle the jamón serrano over the top. Season with salt to taste.
